Welcome to TravelNITT

Your go-to ride-hailing website exclusively designed for NIT Trichy students.

We provide a seamless platform for you to easily connect with local auto rickshaws and the NITT bus service. Our mission is to offer affordable and convenient transportation options by partnering with local auto drivers from around the campus.

Why Choose TravelNITT?

  • Community-Driven: TravelNITT is a community-based initiative, supporting local auto drivers and fostering a sense of community.
  • Affordable Rides: Avoid the high costs of Uber and Ola, which charge exorbitant prices due to long distances. TravelNITT offers budget-friendly transportation options.
  • Convenience: Easily find and contact local auto rickshaws and NITT bus services directly from our platform.

Key Features

  • Direct Contact Information: Access the contact details of local auto drivers and bus services.

  • Real-Time Availability: Get up-to-date information on the availability and timings of NITT buses from the college website.
  • Safe and Reliable: All our drivers are verified and trusted members of the NIT Trichy community.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Our intuitive design makes it easy to find and book your rides quickly.

Additional Features Coming Soon

  • Ride Scheduling: Plan your trips in advance by scheduling rides at your preferred time.
  • Ride Sharing: Connect with fellow students heading in the same direction to share rides and reduce costs.
  • Feedback System: Rate and review drivers to help us maintain high service standards.
  • Emergency Assistance: Quick access to emergency contacts and assistance for any travel-related issues.
  • Mobile App: Stay tuned for our upcoming mobile app for even more convenience on the go.

How It Works

  1. Select Your Ride: Choose between an auto rickshaw or the NITT bus service.
  2. Check Availability: View real-time availability and timings.
  3. Contact the Driver: Directly call the driver using the provided contact details.
  4. Enjoy Your Ride: Experience a safe and affordable journey.
